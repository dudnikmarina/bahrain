Trading history

In 2016, Bahrain and the UK celebrated over 200 years of formal relations since the Treaty of Friendship was first signed in 1816. Since Bahrain became independent from Britain in 1971, the two nations have maintained a strong relationship.

Today there are over 500 active UK commercial agencies and over 90 branches of British companies in Bahrain. In 2016, Bahrain-UK trade (excluding oil) was worth US$526 million.

Travel & Visas

Gulf Air and British Airways fly daily to London with a flight time of 7 hours. And with other Middle Eastern airlines flying to major British cities, it’s never been easier to travel to the Kingdom.

Visas for British nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details.
