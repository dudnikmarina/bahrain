Trading history

Bahrain and South Korea celebrated 40 years of bilateral relations in 2016.

Trade between Bahrain and South Korea has grown steadily recently due to complementary export and import transactions. In 2016, total goods traded (excluding oil) reached US$229 million.

Travel & Visas

Korean Air and Emirates offer indirect daily flights from Bahrain to Seoul.

Visas for South Korean nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details.

Leading South Korean companies have invested in Bahrain, including: Hyundai Engineering and Construction, Hyundai Heavy Industries, Samsung Engineering co. ltd., Woori Bank, Youngjin Enterprise, KEB Hana Bank, GS Engineering & Construction Corp., and LS Cable LTD, S-1 Corporation in partnership with AJM Kooheji Group
