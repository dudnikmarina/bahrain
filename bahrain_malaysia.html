Trading history

Malaysia and Bahrain have had strong diplomatic relations since 2003 and are activating a Joint Commission Meeting, to monitor and implement bilateral agreements. Bilateral trade between Malaysia and Bahrain totalled US$147 million in 2015

Malaysia’s position as an international Islamic financial centre complements Bahrain’s focus in this area – the largest Islamic financial centre in the Gulf, and there are exciting opportunities to promote greater collaboration

Malaysian construction companies have seen particular success in Bahrain. They have supported the construction of the Formula 1 circuit, the North Manama corridor improvement project, the Sitra causeway bridges, and the Bahrain City Centre amongst others.

Travel & Visas

Emirates, Etihad and Qatar Airways offer daily connecting flights to Kuala Lumpur, with a minimum flight time of approximately 10 hours.

Visas for Malaysian nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details
