Trading history

Bahrain and Singapore have had diplomatic relations since 1978 in fact Singapore’s Free Trade Agreement with Bahrain made them the first non-Middle Eastern country to have an FTA with the Gulf.

Bilateral trade between the two countries has increased by 325% since 2004, with a value of US$455 million in 2015 .

Travel & Visas

Gulf Air fly daily to Singapore from Bahrain with a flight time of 10 hours. And with other Middle Eastern airlines offering multiple indirect flights to Singapore every day, it’s never been easier to travel to the Kingdom.

Visas for Singapore nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details.

Many leading Singaporean companies have invested in Bahrain, including: Ascott, BreadTalk, Charles&Keith, CrimsonLogic, Etonhouse, Meinhardt, MTQ, Pico and Vector Infotech.
