Trading history

Bahrain has been connecting the Gulf to the world for thousands of years, with maritime trading between the Gulf and China established as early as the 8th century AD. Evidence of this long relationship can be found at many of the archaeological sites around the Kingdom. The value of trade between Hong Kong and Bahrain increased by 22.8% in 2015, with a value of US$139.43 million.
