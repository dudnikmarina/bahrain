Trading history

Bahrain and Italy have enjoyed diplomatic relations since 2002, when Italy opened their Embassy in the Kingdom.

Today, there are 182 Italian commercial agencies across various sectors, mainly in medicine, pharmaceuticals and fashion. In 2016, Bahrain-Italian trade (excluding oil) was worth US$367.5 million

Travel & Visas

Gulf Air and Alitalia fly daily from Manama to Rome with a flight time of 7 hours. And with other Middle Eastern airlines flying to major Italian cities, it’s never been easier to travel to the Kingdom.

Visas for Italian nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details.
