Trading history

Our bilateral agreement with China will be 30 next year, but our two nations have actually been trading for over 2,000 years – a flourishing partnership. In 2016, trade was worth US$1.6 billion. One Belt, One Road is alive and well in our part of the Gulf.

Travel & Visas

China is never far away. Many airlines offer flights, including Etihad, who offer daily connections between Bahrain and Beijing, Shanghai and several other Chinese cities via Abu Dhabi. Average flight times are 12 hours.

Visas for Chinese nationals are available on arrival. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for details.

Leading Chinese companies have invested in Bahrain, including: China CommService, Huawei, Bank of China, China Harbour Engineering Company Ltd, CPIC, ChinaMex, CIMC, Jinluo Water, East Star Group
