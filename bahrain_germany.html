Trading history

Bahrain and Germany have shared bilateral relations since 1972 and Bahrain EDB are dedicated to promoting this relationship. More recently Bahrain has focused on closer economic relations with German SME’s. In 2016, Bahrain-German trade (excluding oil) was worth US$405 million.

Travel & Visas

Gulf Air flies daily to Frankfurt with a flight time of 7 hours. And with other Middle Eastern airlines flying to major German cities, it’s never been easier to travel to the Kingdom.

Visas for German nationals are available on arrival. There is also an option to apply for an e-visa prior to travelling, simplifying the entry process. Contact the Bahrain Embassy or Consulate for more details.

Over 50 German companies have invested in Bahrain, including: BASF, RMA, DHL, Allianz Global Investors, Armacell, Deutsche Bank, Lufthansa Technik, Schmidt Middle East Logistics
